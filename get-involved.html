Get Involved in 3 Easy Steps

Code for DC is powered by people. If you are an experienced technologist or a fledgling coder, come lend your skill and learn. If you know design, UX, marketing, content strategy or outreach, we need your help. If you're a journalist, welcome! And if you're an advocate in the community who knows where the real problems lie, come tell us what's what.

We meet up every two weeks for civic hack nights - we all get together and work on our ongoing projects and hatch ideas for new ones.
Step One: Join our Meetup group and RSVP.

We talk online. Whether you're interested in finding a project, grokking what we're about, or putting in your two cents about how to help the District, you should join our listserv.
Step Two: Join our Google Group.

So but what can you do now? Check out the list of current projects. You'll see what we're up to already and you might just spot something you'd like to get involved in immediately. OR you might see something missing.
Step Three: Check out our projects. Email the project lead or start your own proejct by emailing Matt or Justin.
