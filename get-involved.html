Get Involved

Code for DC is powered by people. If you are an experienced technologist or a fledgling coder, come lend your skill and learn. If you know design, UX, marketing, content strategy or outreach, we need your help. If you're a journalist, welcome! And if you're an advocate in the community who knows where the real problems lie, come tell us what's what.

We generally meet every two weeks for "civic hack nights," in which we all get together and work on our ongoing projects and hatch new ones. If you're new, come to the next one and see what's up.
